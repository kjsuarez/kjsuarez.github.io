guildHouse

guildHouse is an online platform for playing pen and paper games in the vein of Pathfinder and Dungeons & Dragons. guildHouse wants to make pen-and-paper games easier to play. If you've ever wanted a streamlined interface for playing D&D, or a place to save your character/monster data, or a way to communicate secretly with your players and DM, then guildHouse has somthing for you.
