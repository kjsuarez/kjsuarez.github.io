Kevin Suarez is a world traveler, artist, developer and creator of guildHouse.

When not coding alone in the dark, Kevin can usually be found furiously scribbling in his sketchbook, daydreaming, or walking through the streets of strange cities.

guildHouse is an online platform for playing pen and paper games in the vein of Pathfinder and Dungeons & Dragons. With guildHouse you can share monsters and characters you've created and play D&D long distance.
